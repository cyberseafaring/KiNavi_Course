  <section class="course-section radar-logic">
    <div>
      <p class="course-kicker">Course Logic</p>
      <h2>从回波图像到避碰判断</h2>
      <p>雷达学习的主线是把电磁波、天线、发射接收、显示调节、目标标绘和航行规则连接起来。学生先理解雷达为什么能发现目标，再学习如何调出可靠图像，随后用 EBL、VRM、ARPA、CPA、TCPA 和海图信息形成安全判断。</p>
      <p class="radar-en">The learning path connects electromagnetic principles, bridge controls, radar plotting, target vectors, and COLREGs-based judgement into one operational workflow.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="radar-flow">
      <span><b>01</b> 原理认知<em>Principles</em></span>
      <span><b>02</b> 系统组成<em>System</em></span>
      <span><b>03</b> 图像调节<em>Tuning</em></span>
      <span><b>04</b> 目标标绘<em>Plotting</em></span>
      <span><b>05</b> 避碰决策<em>Decision</em></span>
    </div>
  </section>
